Brand Story

Designed where carbon engineering meets saltwater instinct.

AeroLift began as a small test program for riders who wanted electric assist without losing the purity of foil flight. Every board is organized around the same idea: fewer distractions, better parts, longer sessions, and a ride feel that makes progression obvious from the first lift.
